Know about us

The Urja Shop is steadily growing electronic retail store. Founded in 2002, we bring under one roof, multi-brand digital gadgets and home electronics products.


From large appliances such as Air Conditioners, Refrigerators, and Televisions to handy devices such as Laptops, Mobile Phones , and Cameras, TUS stocks a wide range of products from over 300 brands.


The Urja Shop brings alive the promise of a ‘Brighter Every Day’ with a world-class ambience to shop both in-store and online on our website while also creating a seamless omnichannel shopping experience that lets you enjoy the best of both, online and offline worlds.


You’ll always be spoilt for choice at TUS but will never be left confused. Experts at our stores will help you find the right appliance or gadget for your needs.
